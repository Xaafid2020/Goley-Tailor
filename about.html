About Goley Tailor

Crafting Excellence Since 2003

Our Story

Founded in 2003 by master tailor James Goley, Goley Tailor began as a small neighborhood shop with a big dream: to provide exceptional custom tailoring services that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern style.

What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted name in the fashion industry, serving clients from all walks of life - from business professionals to celebrities, from wedding parties to everyday fashion enthusiasts.

Our commitment to quality, attention to detail, and personalized service has remained unchanged throughout our journey. Every garment that leaves our shop is a testament to our dedication to excellence and our passion for the art of tailoring.

Our Process

1

Consultation

We begin with a detailed consultation to understand your style preferences, needs, and timeline.

2

Measurement

Precise measurements are taken to ensure a perfect fit. We take over 30 measurements for custom suits.

3

Design & Fabric

Choose from our premium fabric selection and finalize design details with our expert guidance.

4

Crafting

Our skilled tailors begin the meticulous process of creating your garment with traditional techniques.

5

Fitting

Multiple fittings ensure perfect fit and comfort. We make adjustments until you're completely satisfied.

6

Final Delivery

Your completed garment is pressed, packaged, and ready for you to enjoy with confidence.
